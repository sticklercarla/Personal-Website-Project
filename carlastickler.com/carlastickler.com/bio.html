Bio
Carla Stickler is a singer, actor, and teacher residing in New York City, best known for her performance as Elphaba on Broadway and in the touring production of Wicked. She is a world traveler having performed throughout Asia and Canada. Carla has headlined as a Guest Entertainer onboard Norwegian Cruise Lines and Disney Cruise Lines.

Carla holds her Masters Degree in Theatre Education in Colleges and Communities through New York University's Steinhardt School of Education. She travels around the United States teaching masterclasses as well as maintaining a private voice studio in Park Slope, Brooklyn. Carla has worked closely with The Educational Theatre Association and spent 4 years attending the International Thespian Festival in Lincoln, NE as a teaching artist and guest performer.

Carla is a proud graduate of Interlochen Arts Academy, New York University's Tisch School of the Arts and Steinhardt School of Education.
